National Grid Future Energy Scenarios

Within this section, the effect that the integration of low carbon technology has on electrical flows at primary substation level is assessed. In particular, scenarios outlined by National Grid, the GB electricity system operator, are analysed in terms of how they may change electrical flows at substations across Scotlandâ€™s electrical system. Four different Future Energy Scenarios may be analysed. Refer to their website and here for more information on these.

Users should select a GSP area of interest and an annual period between 2018 and 2040 to analyse. Users may also select whether Active Network Management strategies and/or Energy Storage flexibility is included across the GSP area. Hourly flow data is output in csv format. If Active Network Management is selected, the amount of curtailed energy at each substation is also output.
