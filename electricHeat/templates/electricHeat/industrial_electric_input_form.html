Industrial Heat Pumps

In this section you can assess the electrical demand in a scenario where a particular percentage of heat demand from industries connected to a Grid Supply Point (GSP) in Scotland is serviced using heat pumps. Industrial Heat demand was modelled for each GSP area using a combination of temperature data and existing industrial gas demand in that area. A heat pump model is used to determine the equivalent reusltant electrical demand. Electrical heat pump demand can either be downloaded to a csv file or plotted to view in your browser. There are various parameters that you should define, including:

  • The GSP of interest.
  • The percentage of industrial heat demand that would be met using heat pumps.
  • A time frame for analysis.

Fill in the fields below and click either download or plot to access the data.
